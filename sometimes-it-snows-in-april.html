Sometimes It Snows In April

Sometimes It Snows in April, 2016

March and April are peak jacaranda season in Mexico City. Back in April 2016 while living in Toronto, I was working on this funky rendering of my former neighborhood when I learned Prince had passed. The jacaranda tree could no longer be depicted intact. Those cascading petals mark the end of a lush purple-tinged life. The 421 is the record of sunset as street address. He was right: Sometimes it snows in April.
